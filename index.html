I was introduced to the internet in the final years of the 1990s, back when the internet screamed. The web back then was clumsy, quirky, and a little cringey; let's simply say I could relate.

Those early days shaped me into the person I am today and the internet has shaped the world as we know it.

This past year, as a Software Development student at MITT, I have learned the tools to shape the internet myself. From sleek front-end design to robust data structures and innovative software, I've gained a wealth of knowledge.

While I'm new to the field, I've been busy putting that knowledge to use. I've built a strong foundation and a growing portfolio that reflects my dedication and progress.

In addition to my recent studies, I bring creativity, a strong work ethic, and a knack for unique problem-solving to every project I take on.
