About

Dave Sommerville (He/Him)

Winnipeg, Mb, Canada

I was introduced to the internet in the final years of the 1990s, back when the internet screamed. Those early days shaped me into the person I am today. The web back then was clumsy, quirky, and a little cringey but it also held unlimited potential. I felt much the same about myself. Prior to beginning my web developer journey I made experiences in a variety of fields, all of which helped me grow in some way.

This past year, I've been diving headfirst into that world, learning new languages and technologies at a rapid pace. I've worked extensively with CSS and JavaScript on personal projects (check out my portfolio below) and am currently building several ASP.NET Entity Framework applications. I bring a mix of robust talents and experiences to the table, and I've taken to the field quickly. For more information, please free to explore my CV or GitHub repositories, interact with the rest of my site, or send me a message!
